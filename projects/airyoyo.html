AIRYO-YO

A playful air purifier designed specifically for tiny apartments.

Project Type Smart Living Device
Duration 5 months
Year 2021-2022
Category Industrial Production

Project Overview

The Concept

Airyo-yo is a playful air purifier designed specifically for tiny apartments. Its distinctive yo-yo-inspired shape adds a touch of fun and irony to the product, while also offering practical functionality.

Designed for industrial production via injection molding, Airyo can be positioned in three different configurations, allowing users to adapt it to the available space in their apartment.

Equipped with both a HEPA filter and an activated carbon filter, Airyo efficiently purifies the air, ensuring a cleaner and healthier environment. Its compact design and versatile placement options make it an ideal solution for small living spaces.

The Research

Air pollution in tiny apartments is a growing concern, as smaller spaces can trap pollutants like dust, bacteria, and VOCs, leading to poor air quality. This can cause health issues such as headaches, fatigue, and impaired concentration, which greatly affect everyday well-being.

The aim of this project was to create a versatile air purifier that could adapt to the limited space available in small apartments, while also adding a playful element to the home environment.

  • Compact design for small spaces
  • Multiple positioning options
  • HEPA and activated carbon filtration
  • Playful yo-yo-inspired design
  • Energy-efficient operation

Product Details

Airyo is designed for industrial production via injection molding, making it highly durable and suitable for mass production. Equipped with both a HEPA filter and an activated carbon filter, it efficiently cleans the air by trapping harmful particles and pollutants.

Once powered on, its brushless motor activates the internal fan, drawing polluted air through the bottom shell. The air then passes through the filters, and finally, clean, purified air is released through the upper shell's diffuser, ensuring a healthier indoor environment.

The Switch

The switch was intentionally placed on the transformer to enhance convenience. With the non-removable cable keeping the transformer near the power outlet, this setup saves users from needing to reach for the purifier itself, which may be wall-mounted or ceiling-hung. This design choice ensures ease of use, perfectly suited for compact living spaces.

A sleek white LED provides instant visual feedback, lighting up when the device is powered on. This simple yet effective feature keeps the design clean and modern while ensuring users always know the status of their air purifier at a glance.

The Configurations

01Surface Mode

The surface configuration lets you place Airyo-yo on any flat surface. From a cozy bedside table to a sleek kitchen counter, this setup provides effortless convenience, making clean air just an arm's reach away, wherever you need it most.

02Ceiling Mode

Transform the space by hanging Airyo-yo from the ceiling, turning air purification into a floating design statement. Ideal for homes with limited wall or floor space.

03Wall Mode

The purifier can be mounted on the wall, where surfaces are limited or if the ceiling height isn't sufficient for the ceiling configuration. This allows the user to maximize available space without compromising functionality.
