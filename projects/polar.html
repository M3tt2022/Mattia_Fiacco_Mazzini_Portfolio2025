POLAR

A smart, wearable ARTVA device designed to improve snow safety.

Project Type Product-Service
Duration 4 months
Year 2023-2024
Category Design for Sport

Project Overview

The Concept

Polar is a smart, wearable ARTVA device designed to improve snow safety. It consists of two parts: the main device, completely redesigned for enhanced usability, and a high-visibility outer band that doubles as a protective cover.

Polar raises awareness about snow safety by making ARTVA more accessible and user-friendly. The device was developed using HP MJF 3D printing technology, allowing for customizable and complex geometries that enhance durability and flexibility.

Polar promotes sustainability by enabling on-demand production, reducing waste, and supporting a more eco-friendly manufacturing approach.

Safety on Snow

Polar is a smart, wearable ARTVA designed to enhance safety while also raising awareness about avalanche risks. Avalanches are a growing danger in snow-covered environments, causing over 150 deaths globally each year.

The combination of unpredictable weather patterns and the rising popularity of off-piste activities has made avalanche preparedness more critical than ever. Snow conditions can change rapidly, and even experienced skiers and mountaineers can be caught off guard.

An ARTVA (Avalanche Rescue Transceiver) locates individuals buried under snow during an avalanche. Its use will soon be mandatory in many high-risk areas, as it greatly enhances survival chances during rescue operations.

  • Enhanced snow safety
  • Wearable design
  • High visibility
  • User-friendly interface
  • Sustainable production

Re-Design & Prototype

By incorporating a high-visibility outer band, I aim to make the device not just functional but also eye-catching. This visibility encourages greater interest in avalanche safety and helps foster a culture of preparedness among outdoor enthusiasts.

The Polar ARTVA features a fully redesigned main unit for ease of use and is built with HP MJF 3D printing technology, offering durability and flexibility, along with sustainable, on-demand production.

Technical Details

The MJF technology allows for the creation of custom-made wristbands for each user, offering the option to personalize not only the text on the band but also the diameter according to the arm's circumference.

The device is fully disassemblable, allowing easy replacement of electronic components. POLAR is also waterproof, thanks to a high-quality sealing gasket, ensuring durability and maintainability in extreme conditions.

Connection Point + Service

Workshop

The service accompanying Polar consists of a series of workshops held on snow-covered terrain and ski slopes. These workshops focus on increasing awareness of snow safety, practical hands-on sessions, and training on how to properly use the ARTVA device.

Additionally, there are sports activities with rewards, encouraging participants to actively engage with and apply their knowledge of ARTVA use in real-world scenarios.

App

Through the design of a dedicated app, users can further customize the length and color of their ARTVA device, ensuring a tailored fit and style that suits their individual needs.
