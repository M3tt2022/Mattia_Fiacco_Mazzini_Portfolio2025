About Me

Download my CV Download my PORTFOLIO

I am a student of Integrated Product Design at Politecnico di Milano, born and raised in Milan.

I consider myself a young man with a strong creative approach, enriched by a high level of curiosity which, in my conception of design, is essential to understand and satisfy users' needs.

I am characterized by two approaches: a highly creative one that I explored and defined through my high school background, and a search for the functionality of the things that surround us, an aspect that I have been able to deepen during these years at the Politecnico di Milano.

My approach in doing design is to always challenge myself to find the perfect balance between design rationality and the formal aesthetics of products.

I am interested in Strategic and Service Design, scenario analysis, Concept Design and all phases of product development and engineering.
